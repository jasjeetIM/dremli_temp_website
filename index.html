Mission

Automate the path from patient sign-up to paid claim.

Dental practices should not need a full-time claims role to keep revenue moving. Dremli is built to run the repetitive insurance work across eligibility, claims, status checks, and payment follow-up, with staff pulled in only when a real exception needs a decision.

Customer

Independent and multi-location dental practices that want reliable collections without adding more back-office headcount.

Problem

Insurance work lives across the PMS, payer portals, clearinghouse tools, spreadsheets, and phone calls until it becomes a full-time administrative job.

Solution

Dremli connects the insurance workflow into one automation layer that verifies benefits, submits claims, checks status, and follows the payment cycle.

Outcome

Front-office and clinical teams stay focused on patients while routine revenue-cycle work keeps moving in the background.

Automation

What Dremli takes off the team's plate.

Dremli is designed around the full dental revenue cycle, not a single task queue. Each step stays tied to the same patient, payer, provider, and claim context.

  1. Patient sign-up
  2. Eligibility
  3. Benefit capture
  4. Claim creation
  5. Claim submission
  6. Status checks
  7. Payment follow-up

The system does the repeatable work automatically and turns the rest into an exception queue, so the practice only sees the items that need judgment instead of reconstructing every claim by hand.

Why Dremli

Built for the whole insurance loop, not another inbox.

Practices do not need another dashboard that still depends on staff to chase every payer. Dremli's advantage is an end-to-end workflow that automates routine revenue-cycle labor and clearly escalates the few cases that need human review.

  • End-to-end context from intake through payment
  • Dental-specific claim and payer workflows
  • Exception-first queues for work that actually needs staff
  • Tenant isolation, encrypted data, and auditable state changes
